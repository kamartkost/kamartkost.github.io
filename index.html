Hummus

  • Time: 15mins
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Chickpeas 200g
Tahini 1 tbsp
Olive oil 1 tbsp

Step 1: Wash chickpeas.
Step 2: Add all ingredients to a food processor.
Step 3: Pulse blend until smooth.
Step 4: Serve.

Flat Bread

  • Time: 10mins
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200g
Water 100 g
Olive oil 1 tbsp

Step 1: Combine flour and water.
Step 2: Mix until solid using hands.
Step 3: Pull small handfulls of dough and then roll flat.
Step 4: Put a small amount of oil into a hot pan and cook the bread on both sides.

Tahini Sauce

  • Time: 10mins
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Garlic 2 cloves
Lemon Juice 1 lemon
Tahini 1 tbsp
Water 6 tbsp

Step 1: Squeeze juice from the lemon and add garlic once finely chopped. Allow to sit for 30 minutes.
Step 2: Remove garlic using a strainer.
Step 3: Add the tahini and mix until it becomes a paste.
Step 4: Add spoons of cold water, 2 at a time, until smooth.
